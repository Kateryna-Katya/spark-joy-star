Welcome to Spark Joy Star

Inspiring young minds to shine and create a brighter future.

At Spark Joy Star, we believe that every child has unique talents waiting to be discovered.

Our center provides a creative and nurturing environment where children explore their potential in arts, music, science, and leadership.

Through hands-on experiences, innovative teaching, and expert mentorship, we guide them toward a future full of possibilities.

Our Philosophy

Every child is a star.
Our mission is to help them shine.

Passion-Driven Learning:
We encourage kids to explore their interests through interactive, experience-based learning.
Confidence & Leadership:
Our programs build self-esteem, communication skills, and teamwork abilities from an early age
Safe & Supportive Space:
Every child feels welcomed, encouraged, and free to express themselves in our learning center.
Holistic Development:
We focus on creativity, emotional intelligence, and problem-solving skills to prepare children for the future.
